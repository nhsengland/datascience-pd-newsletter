Analysis without Data

Tuesday 21st May, 12-1pm, Online

This session outlines work by the Ministry of Justice (MoJ)‚Äôs Better Outcomes through Linked Data (BOLD) programme Homelessness Pilot colleagues in Department for Levelling Up, Housing and Communities (DLUHC). The Better Outcomes through Linked Data programme aims to demonstrate the benefits of linked data across government. In DLUHC‚Äôs Homelessness Pilot Team, our challenge was a lack of full data access for sensitivity reasons.
